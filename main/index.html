The World

This information is still up in the air, but probably won't be changed too much

Some day I'll come up with a better name than The World, for now, it's not a high priority.

If you're on this webpage it probably means you're going to be playing in my campaign, this website is designed to give you information that'll be relevent to character creation, and information about The World. If I manage to do things right, you'll porbably want to check this somewhat often as it'll keep track of information for you (in case you forget!).

Information

  • Ruleset: 3.5 mixed with Pathfinder... or Pathfinder, I'm not 100% sure yet
  • We're starting at 4th level
  • Race selection can be found at Races. If you want to play anything not listed there you'll have to discuss it with me
    • Sub-race flavors like Whisper Gnome and Strongheart Halfling are Okay
  • 28 (+1 from level four) point buy, 1:1 for stat points
  • Standard wealth by level (6,000 GP @ 4)
  • Skill Changes:
    • Hide and Move Silents are combined into one skill: Stealth
    • Spot, Listen, and Search are combined into one skill: Perception
  • Planned start date is 6/7/2013 @ 7:00 pm
